Register your preference for sharing health data

Register your preference

For security reasons we must verify who you are before you can set your preference.

We will do this by searching for your contact details in our database in order to send you a one-time verification code.

How the service works

1

Check your details

Tell us your name, date of birth, postcode and NHS number. We can then find your contact information in our patient database.

The details you provide will not be saved.

2

Confirm your identity

Using the details we hold in our database, we will send you a one-time verification code to your selected contact method.

Enter your verification code online to prove your identity.

3

Set your preference

You will then be able to set an opt-out preference. The decision you make will not affect your individual care.

You can also change your mind at any time.

By continuing you agree to let us search for your details in our database to send you a one-time security passcode.

Agree and continue
