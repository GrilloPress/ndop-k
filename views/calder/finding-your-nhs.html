Finding your NHS number

To use this service, you will be required to provide your NHS number.

If you are not sure where to find this, there are a few places you can check:

A letter from the NHS

You should be able to find your NHS Number on any letter or document you have received from the NHS, including test results, and hospital referral or appointment letters.

Your NHS number will usually be located at the top of the page.

A prescription

You can find your NHS Number on a prescription you have received from the NHS.

Your NHS number is located on the green side of the prescription, which you would hand to a pharmacist to obtain your medication.

Speak to your GP surgery

You can find your NHS Number on a prescription you have received from the NHS.

Your NHS number is located on the green side of the prescription, which you would hand to a pharmacist to obtain your medication.
